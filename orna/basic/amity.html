Amity

General Info

  1. Amity seeds depend on 3 things: Party, Hour and the final node Q&A.

  2. The seed is what determines the Amity bonus + malus.

  3. Rarity generally changes the maluses - the main bonus will always remain.

  4. Rarity is rolled for each individual hunt, but the same rarity will have all the same bonuses + maluses for the seed (people will receive the same legendary or ornate Amity).

  5. Some bonuses + maluses are not available to all tiers, so different tiers usually get different Amities from the same seed.

  6. Seed hour blocks repeat every 24 hours on UTC - so the same seed repeats every day at the same hour until weekly rotation.

  7. Seeds last weekly and rotate at the same time as dungeons (00:00 UTC).

  8. IRL location doesn’t matter as long as the above conditions are met!

  9. You can start a memory hunt and complete 3/5 memories before joining a party to receive the same seed at the final node Q&A. You must complete the 4th memory hunt in the correct party to generate the same seeded final nodes.

  10. When hunting Amities, to always be in a party is recommended! If you’re not in a party and find a good Amity you want to share, when you invite people it will create a new party and change the seed.

Very Important Amities

  1. Critical hits will be 40% more effective

  2. Positive status effects will last 50% longer

  3. You will gain 5% additional view distance

  4. You will receive 100% additional rewards when completing memories

  5. Your accessories will be 25% more effective

Frequently Used Amities

  1. Defending in battle will reduce an additional 75% damage

  2. Increases arcane damage by 30%

  3. Increases dragon damage by 30%

  4. Increases dark damage by 20%

  5. Increases earthen damage by 20%

  6. Increases fire damage by 20%

  7. Increases holy damage by 20%

  8. Increases lightning damage by 20%

  9. Increases water damage by 20%

  10. Simple attacks will deal 40% more damage

  11. You have a 20% chance to be protected from attacks that would deal 100% of your maximum HP

  12. You will begin battle with 5 ward turns activated

  13. You will recover 5% of your maximum HP per turn

  14. You will recover 5% of your maximum mana per turn

  15. You will recover 5% of your missing HP per turn

  16. You will recover 5% of your missing Mana per turn

  17. You will recover 5% ward each turn

  18. Your follower is 7% more likely to protect you in battle

  19. Your follower’s stats will be increased by 15%

  20. Your summons will be 30% stronger

Amity Sharing may be the most social part in Orna.
