Beastfelled

Sword or no sword, staff or no staff. To don a suit of armor or leave ourselves vulnerable. Trials await us.

SzHb’s Notes

Goodies

  1. ★10 Weapon - Arisen Feral Archistaff

    • (Magic users) 7% follower act

    • From ★10 raid Arisen Kerberos

    • For horde and dual-wielding.

  2. ★10 Off-hand - Arisen Wolven Tower Shield

    • High Def/Res

    • From ★10 raid Arisen Kerberos

    • For turtle build

  3. ★5/9 Weapon Adorn - Dark / Blackened Eye of Kerberos

    • 2% Follower Act, 2% ward (superior+), overall stats boost (blackened)

    • From ★5/9 boss Kin of Kerberos

    • For horde and endless

  4. ★10 Torso - Beastfelled Garb

    • Grant your follower or summons Berserk skill

    • From ★10 raid Turned King Lyncus

    • For horde and Realmshifter in raid

  5. ★10 Armor Adorn - Monoceros Essence

    • Avidity + 1% (max 30%)

    • From ★8 monster Great Monoceros

    • For Realmshifter in tower and horde

  6. ★5 Follower - Kin of Kerberos

    • 4 faction elements Alignment

    • From Bestiary and Monumental Guild

    • Good Ultima pet

Tips

  1. You will need at lest 10 superior+ Blackened Eye.

Quests

  1. Win a battle by exploiting an elemental weakness (5)

    • Reward: 5k gold, 1k orns

  2. Win a battle against a boss of your tier while only exploiting elemental weaknesses (2)

    • Reward: 6.5k gold, 1.2k orns

  3. Defeat enemies with a critical hit (5)

    • Reward: 10k gold, 2k orns

  4. Win battle of six or more rounds against foes of your tier without taking damage (3)

    • 12k gold, 2.5k orns

  5. Win a battle against a boss of your tier without an equipped weapon (5)

    • 50k gold, 5k orns

  6. Finish a battle dungeon of your tier without wearing any armor (1)

    • Reward: 150k gold, 7k orns

  7. Finish a World Raid of any tier alone in one battle (1)

    • Reward: 500k gold, 15k orns

  8. Find: Manticore Heart (10)

    • Reward: 2.5m gold, 25k orns, Lyncus' Ring
