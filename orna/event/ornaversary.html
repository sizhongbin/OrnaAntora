Ornaversary

It’s Orna’s birthday! Enjoy extra experience, orns, gold, and double the dungeon rewards.

SzHb’s Notes

Goodies

  1. ★6 Off-hand - Pizza (Resprite of Bricriu’s Bow)

    • Give Arrowstorm off-hand skill

    • From ★6 Raid MmmPizza

    • For horde

  2. ★9 Torso - Dangy’s Fancy T-shirt (Resprite of Fey Yeti Coat)

    • Ward + HP + Mana

    • From ★7 Raid Dangy

    • Good generic chest

  3. ★10 Accessory - Dangy’s House Key (Resprite of Carl’s Ring)

    • Overall stat boosting accessory

    • From ★7 Raid Dangy

    • Good for Spiked/Chained Shield build

  4. ★10 Weapon - Banhammer (Resprite of Finesse’s Greatsword)

    • (Warrior) High attack, high ward, 12 slots

    • From ★7 Raid Dangy

    • For Ultimastrikes build

  5. ★10 Off-hand - Dangy’s Laptop (Resprite of Spiked Greatshield)

    • 1.2x ward power

    • From ★7 Raid Dangy

    • Good for SS/CS build

  6. ★10 Torso - Covyn’s Dark Armor (Resprite of Arisen Pumpkinless Armor)

    • Low def, high HP, 2H bonus

    • From ★10 Raid Arisen Covyn

    • Good BoF gear

  7. ★10 Leg - Covyn’s Dark Leggings (Resprite of Arisen Pumpkinless Leggings)

    • Low def, high HP, 2H bonus

    • From ★10 Raid Arisen Covyn

    • Good BoF gear

  8. ★10 Head - Argos

    • Resprite of Arisen Cursed Fomor’s Crown for all classes, with 20% VD and Defend Power

    • From ★10 Raid The Creator

    • Good VD gear
